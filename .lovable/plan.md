## Core Features

### 1. Idea Capture & Dashboard
- **Visual idea cards** displayed in a colorful, Trello-like board layout
- Multiple ways to add ideas: quick capture, detailed entry, file upload, or paste
- Color-coded categories and visual tags for easy scanning
- Search across all your ideas instantly

### 2. AI Brainstorming Partner
The AI will proactively help you develop ideas:
- **Automatic refinement suggestions** when you add a new idea
- **"What if..." prompts** to explore different angles
- **Next steps recommendations** - what to do to move the idea forward
- **Connection alerts** - "This relates to your other idea about..."
- **Tool recommendations** - suggest which of your tools could help develop this idea

### 3. Smart Organization
- **Automatic topic clustering** - AI groups related ideas together
- **Visual boards** organized by theme, project, or priority
- **Link ideas together** manually or let AI suggest connections
- **Timeline view** to see your idea evolution

### 4. Import Center
- **File upload** supporting .txt, .md, .docx, .pdf
- **Paste area** for quick copy/paste from Notes, Docs, etc.
- **AI parsing** - automatically extracts key points from imported content and suggests how to organize them

### 5. Reminder & Revival System
- **Set staleness alerts** - get reminded if an idea hasn't been touched in X days
- **Weekly digest** - summary of ideas that need attention
- **AI nudges** - "You haven't worked on your podcast idea in 2 weeks. Here are 3 ways to move it forward..."

### 6. Idea Development Workspace
When you click into an idea, you get:
- Full brainstorming chat with AI
- Notes section
- Related ideas sidebar
- Suggested tools & resources
- Progress tracking
